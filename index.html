AKAN NAMES

Akan names(or Personal names in Ghana) consists of several given names and surnames based on the language of ethnic groups in Ghana . Frequently, children are given a "day name" which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.Find yours to day : click to know more about Akan system of naming
